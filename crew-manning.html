Crew Manning & Management

We Take Special Care for your Bussiness

We always believe that the evaluation of the crew selection is very important for the safe operation of the vessel if they get onboard. In our company, we have well defined Human Resource Management (HRM) practices and best selection process.

Offer floating: At first, we circulate the offer among our own database mariners and outside of our database mariners. We call an application from mariners for the desired post through our own company management system (Management software for our company)

CV screening: We screening all CVs from our management system without any biasness.

Oral examination:After screening all the CVs, we call all our primary selected candidates for face to face viva. We evaluate their professional knowledge by internal and external maritime experts i.e. Experienced Masters and Chief Engineers. At the same time, we evaluate their ethics by an experienced physiologist. We focus on the below mentioned key points:

• Professional Knowledge

• Competency

• English fluency

• IQ (Quick problem solving capability & decision taking ability during emergency)

• Technology orientation

• Peer friendly

• Ethics

• Anti-racism mentality

• Good behavior and attitud
